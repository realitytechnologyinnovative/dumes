Affordable Housing Scheme

Wuse Housing Scheme provides quality 2-3 bedroom homes for low and middle-income families in Wuse Zone 4, Abuja. This strategic location offers easy access to the city center while maintaining affordability.

This project demonstrates our commitment to bridging Nigeria's housing deficit by providing quality, affordable homes for families across income levels.

Project Details

  • Location: Wuse Zone 4, Abuja, FCT
  • Type: Affordable Housing
  • Status: Completed
  • Units: 150
  • Price Range: ₦6M - ₦12M
